Flatten

final case class Flatten[NodeT, MatchT, EGraphT <: EGraphLike[NodeT, EGraphT] with EGraph[NodeT]](searcher: Searcher[NodeT, Seq[Traversable[MatchT]], EGraphT]) extends Searcher[NodeT, Seq[MatchT], EGraphT] with Product with Serializable

A searcher that flattens the output of another searcher.

NodeT

The type of the nodes in the e-graph.

MatchT

The type of the matches.

EGraphT

The type of the e-graph that the searcher searches in.

searcher
