StochasticRuleApplication

Companion object StochasticRuleApplication

final case class StochasticRuleApplication[NodeT, RuleT <: Rule[NodeT, MatchT, _], EGraphT <: EGraphLike[NodeT, EGraphT] with EGraph[NodeT], MatchT](rules: Seq[RuleT], searchAndApply: SearchAndApply[NodeT, RuleT, EGraphT, MatchT], priorities: MatchPriorities[NodeT, RuleT, MatchT], random: Random) extends Strategy[EGraphT, Random] with Product with Serializable

A strategy that applies a sequence of rules in a stochastic manner. It searches for matches of the rules in an e-graph, prioritizes them based on a given function, and applies a batch of matches selected randomly, weighing the selection by their priority.

NodeT

The type of the nodes in the e-graph.

RuleT

The type of the rules to apply.

EGraphT

The type of the e-graph.

MatchT

The type of the matches produced by the rules.

rules

The rules to apply.

searchAndApply

The search and apply strategy to find and apply matches.

priorities

The prioritizer that determines the priority of matches and the batch size to apply.

random

A random number generator used for selecting matches randomly.
